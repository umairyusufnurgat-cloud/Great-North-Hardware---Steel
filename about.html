About Us

Great North Hardware & Steel Ltd is your trusted partner in building, construction, and home improvement. Based in Chibombo, Zambia, we provide a wide range of high-quality hardware, steel, and construction materials to meet the needs of contractors, businesses, and individuals alike.

Our Mission

To deliver reliable and affordable hardware and steel products, ensuring our customers get the strength and durability they need for every project.

Our Values

We believe in integrity, customer satisfaction, and building strong relationships with our community and partners.

Our Team

Our experienced and dedicated team is always ready to provide expert advice and support to help you choose the right materials for your projects.
