About Us

Who We Are

Great North Hardware & Steel Ltd has been proudly serving the community for over 15 years with quality building materials, hardware, and steel products. Our mission is to support builders, craftsmen, and homeowners by providing reliable products and expert advice.

From small DIY projects to large commercial constructions, we have the expertise and inventory to meet your needs. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us the trusted choice for thousands of customers.
